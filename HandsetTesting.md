# Glossary #

  * dev - Latest development version (probably unreleased, not stable)
  * S40 / S60 - Series 40 or Series 60 Nokia Phone OS
  * BT - Bluetooth, IR = Infrared, Cable = USB connection, OTA = Over the air (download)
  * Wifi - wireless network
